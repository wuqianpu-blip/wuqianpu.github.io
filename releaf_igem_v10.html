Background

Enormous quantities of agricultural and garden waste, such as straw, corn cobs and landscaping debris, are generated. Traditional incineration releases PM2.5 and dioxins, leading to air pollution, whereas landfilling occupies valuable land and prevents the recovery of organic matter, ultimately resulting in a dual waste of resources and environmental potential.
